    Lake Oahe
    One of the top walleye fisheries in the nation — massive Missouri River reservoir. Spring & fall trolling with crawler harnesses and jigs.
    🐟
    Lake Sharp
    Excellent walleye action on the Missouri River system. Try jig & minnow combos along rock points and current breaks.
    💡
    Walleye Tips
    Dawn & dusk are prime. Use 1/4–3/8 oz jigs with leeches in summer. Troll crankbaits at 1.5–2.5 mph along drop-offs in 10–20 ft.
