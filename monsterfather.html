The Origin Story

He Was
Just a Photographer

Todd started out like any photographer — a camera, some lights, a van full of gear, and a whole lot of Minnesota to shoot. Then something happened. Nobody's entirely sure what. Some say it was a full moon over Lake Superior. Some say it was one too many late-night holiday shoots.

Whatever the cause, the monster emerged. And it turns out the monster takes incredible photos. Themed pin-ups, holiday shoots, glam horror, custom concepts — the creature behind the lens has range.

Fangs and all, still based in Minneapolis. Still answering emails.
