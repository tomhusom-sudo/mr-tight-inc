Honest Sourcing

We only carry what we can trace. Every item on our list comes from a farm or supplier we've personally vetted. No mystery origins, no shrink-wrapped surprises. If we don't know where it came from, it doesn't come through our door.

💪
Healthy Foundations

Good food is the foundation of everything. We stock produce that's in-season, meats raised without unnecessary additives, and baked goods made from real ingredients you can read without a dictionary. Fuel your family right.

🤝
Fair Trade

We pay growers fairly and price honestly for our community. No shell games, no bait-and-switch markups. When farmers thrive, the food gets better for everyone. That's a deal we'll take every time.

Our Promise

What Honest Means to Us

These aren't marketing words. They're how we make decisions every single week.

📋
Full Transparency

Ask us where anything came from and we'll tell you — farm name, state, method. If we can't answer, we don't carry it.

🚜
Local First

We prioritise South Dakota and regional farmers whenever season and quality allow. Shorter supply chain, fresher product.

⚖️
Fair Pricing

We price to cover costs and run a sustainable business — not to maximise margins. If the price goes up, we'll tell you exactly why.

🌍
Fair Trade Certified

All imported goods — coffee, chocolate, tropical fruit — carry verified Fair Trade certification. Workers and growers get paid properly.

🌾
No Unnecessary Additives

We read every label. Meat is meat, bread is bread. If there's a chemical in there that doesn't need to be, it stays off our shelves.

♻️
Zero Waste Mindset

Pre-orders mean we source only what's needed. Surplus goes to local food pantries, not dumpsters.
